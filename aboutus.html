Know Who We Are

First Choice Education Pty Ltd T/A International Institute of Education, RTO No: 45150 And CRICOS: 03838G is a Registered Training Organisation located at 16-18 Wentworth Street Parramatta NSW 2150.

IIE operates under the VET Quality Framework. This is a regulated framework administered by the Australian Skills Quality Authority (ASQA). Our registration details are located on the National Register for VET and our qualifications are recognised under the Australian Qualifications Framework.

As a registered training organisation delivering courses to international students, IIE is also required to maintain registration in compliance with the Education Services for Overseas Students Act 2000 (ESOS Act 2000) and The National Code of Practice for Providers of Education and Training to Overseas Students 2018 (National Code 2018).

IIE offers courses in Early Childhood Education and Care, Accounting, Business, Information Technology, Hospitality, Community Service, Ageing Support, Disability, and Mental Health.
