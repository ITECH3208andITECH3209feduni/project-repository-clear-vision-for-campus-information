Introduction to Accounting

Introduction: This course provides a comprehensive introduction to accounting principles and practices, focusing on financial reporting, bookkeeping, and analysis.

Duration: 12 weeks

Course Objectives: To understand the fundamental principles of accounting, prepare financial statements, and analyze financial data.

Learning Outcomes: Students will be able to create accurate financial reports, manage budgets, and perform financial analysis.

Assessment: Assignments, quizzes, and a final exam. Practical case studies and project work will also be included.

Fees Structure: $1,200

Career Options: Accountant, Financial Analyst, Auditor. Potential roles include working in corporate finance, accounting firms, or government agencies.

Practical Applications: Skills gained can be applied in bookkeeping, financial management, and regulatory compliance within various organizations.

Introduction to Arts

Introduction: This course explores various art forms and creative techniques, including visual arts, sculpture, and multimedia art.

Duration: 10 weeks

Course Objectives: To develop an understanding of different art styles, techniques, and the historical context of art.

Learning Outcomes: Students will gain the ability to create art pieces, critique artworks, and understand the evolution of art.

Assessment: Projects, presentations, and participation. Students will create their own art pieces and participate in group critiques.

Fees Structure: $950

Career Options: Art Director, Gallery Curator, Illustrator. Opportunities exist in art galleries, studios, and creative agencies.

Practical Applications: Knowledge gained can be used in creating art, managing art exhibitions, and developing art-based projects.

Introduction to Business

Duration: 14 weeks

Course Objectives: To provide a broad understanding of business operations, management strategies, and market dynamics.

Learning Outcomes: Students will be able to manage business operations, develop marketing strategies, and analyze financial statements.

Assessment: Case studies, exams, and group projects. Practical assignments will involve real-world business scenarios.

Fees Structure: $1,500

Career Options: Business Manager, Entrepreneur, Market Analyst. Potential careers include roles in corporate management, consultancy, and startups.

Practical Applications: Skills are applicable in business management, strategic planning, and market analysis across various industries.

Introduction: This course covers the basics of legal systems and principles, focusing on contract law, criminal law, and legal procedures.

Duration: 15 weeks

Course Objectives: To provide a foundation in legal principles, the structure of legal systems, and the application of laws.

Learning Outcomes: Students will understand legal concepts, analyze legal issues, and apply legal principles to case studies.

Practical Applications: Skills can be used in legal practice, compliance, and legal advisory roles in various sectors.
